Common Clustering Methods

Prototype-Based Clustering (K-means)

Points belong to the cluster with the nearest centroid

K-means: Iteratively assigns points to K cluster centers (centroids) and updates centroids based on assigned points

Graph-Based Clustering

Clusters are connected components in a graph where edges connect nearby points

Density-Based Clustering (DBSCAN)

Clusters are regions of high density separated by regions of low density

Cluster Analysis for Data Mining
